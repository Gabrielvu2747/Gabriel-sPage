Matchbox Mazda MX-30

The Mazda MX-30 is an electric SUV that resembles the appearance of a coupe. It is a fancy looking car, however, the disappointing part is that it can only go 100 miles on a single range. This version, made by Matchbox, is the moving parts version, where the trunk opens. There are 2 variants, even though both of them have the same body color. These 2 variants are a sleeping dog and a luggage. These both exist inside of the trunk. I only have the sleeping dog version. This car in real life costs from $33,470-$36,480
